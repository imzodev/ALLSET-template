## The Landing Page Design Dilemma

Traditionally, designing a landing page involved a complex process:

- **Market Research:** Understanding your target audience and their needs.
- **Design and Layout:** Creating a visually appealing and user-friendly layout.
- **Copywriting:** Crafting compelling headlines, body text, and calls to action.
- **A/B Testing:** Experimenting with different elements to optimize performance.

This process often requires a team of designers, copywriters, and marketers, making it expensive and time-consuming. For small businesses and startups with limited resources, this can be a significant barrier to entry.
